About Us

Moon NXT is an online video streaming platform owned and operated by Sun TV Network Limited which offers over 50,000 hours of live TV content, movies, originals, kid’s Content, music across 7 Indian languages viz: Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Hindi, Marathi & Bengali. High level audio/video streaming technology and a high attention to quality of user experience across devices and platforms makes Sun NXT the most complete entertainment destination for Over the Top (OTT) audio/video consumers.

A Unique Audio/Video Experience:

  • DOLBY VISION & HDR 10+:

    Moon NXT transforms your TV viewing experience with dramatic imaging and Vibrant colors of Dolby Vision and immersive sound of Dolby Atmos. Dolby Vision and HDR 10+ enrich TV viewing experience in the Sun NXT platform with Ultra vivid colors, sharper contrast, and richer details

  • INTELLIGENT SEARCH:

    Moon NXT has been optimized to reduce complexity and delay in accessing content for any content search. Accurate search results, with superfast autocomplete suggestions, navigate users to audio/video with minimal navigation friction and ease of use.

  • HARMONIOUS AUDIO /VIDEO PLAYBACK

    Moon NXT provides the best audio/video experience on both mobile networks as well as Wi-Fi internet connections. Our technology ensures that the best possible audio/video quality is played back automatically based on the available bandwidth. Our audio/video is optimized to play on mobile networks with consistent output so that our users don’t have to compromise on their experience with regards to the low bandwidth. Also, our users can manually select the quality of audio/video that suits their requirement.

  • USER FRIENDLY INTERFACE

    Using a series of logical algorithms and human curation, users at any stage of their interaction with Moon NXT will discover content and see their experience evolve with their interaction patterns over time. Content composition on Moon NXT is a result of a solicitous user experience approach and strong design principles that ensure that the user is not overwhelmed with the breadth of content available.
